The Timeless Art of Aari Work

Rich Heritage

Aari work is a traditional form of embroidery originating from India, known for its intricate needlework using a hooked needle (aari). This exquisite craft has been passed down through generations, maintaining its cultural significance while evolving with contemporary fashion trends.

Ancient Origins

Dates back to Mughal era, originally used for royal garments

Unique Tools

Special hooked needle and wooden frame used for precision

Modern Applications

Adapted for bridal wear, designer outfits, and home decor

Global Appeal

Recognized worldwide as premium Indian craftsmanship
